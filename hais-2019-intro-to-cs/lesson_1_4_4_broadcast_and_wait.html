Lesson 1.4.4: Animation - Broadcast and Wait

  • What if we want to continue on once all the items are done?
  • Broadcast and Wait Definition
    • Sends a message to all the sprites and the stage AND waits until they are all finished to continue
    • Also works in conjunction with "When I receive"
  • Broadcast and Wait Example I 'broadcast and wait' 'fly' to all sprites. Wait for each bat to fly before starting again.
  • What are real life examples of Broadcast and Wait?
    • Airplain announcements - Wait until that group boards the plane until announcing next group
    • Mom yelling your name - She will be waiting for response before moving on
    • Bus picking up students at different stops - Wait for all kids to load before driving off
    • Class Rollcall - Wait till you raise your hand before going to next name
    • Often we want to wait for a task to complete before continuing
  • Where in software have we seen this?
    • Starting multiplayer video game matches
    • Software that is loading
    • Websites any time you see a loading indicator - Downloads all information before displaying it.
    • Transcoding video on youtube - will finish transpiling each part before it is considered 'finished'
    • Testing code and generating a report on quality
